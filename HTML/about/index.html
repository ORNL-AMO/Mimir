About Mimir

What is Mimir?

Mimir is an open source dashboard that will read in live data from energy equipment, do efficiency calculations, and show efficiency levels in three different readings: one showing the current reading, another showing a more efficient curve given the current equipment, and the last one showing the optimal curve for the most energy efficiency. The AMO-Tools Suite created by the Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL) is used to calculate the efficiencies. The AMO-Tools Suite, also called MEASUR, is a project for the Department of Energy.

Who created it?

Mimir was created by Quentin Johnson, Mariah Mensah, Austin Stephens, Christopher Nagy, Miranda Cheatham, and Michael Colby, six computer science students from Tennessee Technological University.

Who is it for?

Mimir is intended for any manufacturing professional who wants to check the efficiency of their pumps and potentially save money and energy.
